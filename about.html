Professional Profile

About Me

Hi, I’m Vaibhav Makvana, a web developer who enjoys building clean, functional, and user-friendly web applications.

I focus on turning ideas into practical digital products using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend technologies like Node.js, Express, PHP, and databases. I care about writing readable code, building responsive layouts, and creating solutions that actually solve real problems.

What I Do

I build websites and web applications for individuals, startups, and small businesses. My work focuses on clarity, performance, and functionality.

  • → Business and portfolio websites
  • → Landing pages focused on clarity and performance
  • → Custom software like billing, inventory, and hotel systems
  • → Full-stack web applications with authentication and databases

Along with development, I also understand basic SEO and digital presence, which helps websites perform better beyond just looking good.

My Journey

I started learning web development out of curiosity about how websites work and gradually moved into building real projects. I am currently a Diploma Computer Engineering student, which has helped me strengthen my fundamentals in programming, problem-solving, and software development alongside practical project work.

I’m currently expanding my backend knowledge, improving my understanding of authentication, APIs, and scalable project structure, while continuing to strengthen my frontend fundamentals.

How I Work

When I build something, I focus on practical solutions over over-engineering and always try to learn something new from every project.

  • • Understanding the problem before writing code
  • • Keeping the design simple and usable
  • • Writing clean and maintainable code
  • • Making sure the website works well on all devices

What I’m Looking For

I’m open to freelance projects, internship opportunities, or collaborating with startups and small teams. If you have an idea, a project, or a problem that needs a web solution, I’d be happy to discuss it.
