Shree Ganesh Enterprise

Project Overview

Shree Ganesh Enterprise is a precision manufacturing company. This project focuses on presenting their mould accessory products clearly, building trust through professional design, and generating business leads using an inquiry-based quote system rather than direct online payments.

What I Built

✔
Multi-page Website: Home, About, Products, Process, and Contact pages.
✔
Structured Catalog: Designed for easy browsing of industrial mould accessories.
✔
Quote Request System: Integrated forms for B2B inquiries and lead generation.
✔
WhatsApp Integration: Instant communication for immediate business support.
✔
Performance First: Optimized using Tailwind CSS and Vite for lightning-fast loads.

What I Learned

Focused on building a performance-optimized B2B frontend, structuring a multi-page business site for SEO, and designing an industrial product catalog for maximum clarity and lead generation. Gained experience in creating professional brand identities for manufacturing firms.
